Welcome to the Best Brain Academy (BBA), Lagos, a warm, vibrant and successful co-educational school situated in the heart of Victoria Island. The Best Brain academy is located within a spacious, green and tranquil environment. It provides students with an education rooted in the British National Curriculum but blended with key elements of the Nigerian provision at KS3. This is an environ where students will be exposed to differents ways of extracting and analysing datas using tools like excel among others.
